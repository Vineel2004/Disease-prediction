About Our Project

Our project, Virtual Health Assistance Using Machine Learning, is an intelligent system designed to predict diseases based on user-reported symptoms. By integrating multiple ML models and a clean, interactive UI, the system helps users understand their possible health condition and also find nearby hospitals for further assistance.
